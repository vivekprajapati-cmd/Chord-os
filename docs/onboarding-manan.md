## What ChordOS is

ChordOS is the internal ops workspace for 1702 + Chord. It's where all your work lives — what's assigned to you, what's due, and the full brief for each deliverable. No more chasing Pierre or Pratik for "what did the client actually want."

## Your access level

You are a **staff** member. You can:
- See and update your own tasks
- Mark task status as you move through work
- View your calendar blocks
- Read the brand brain for any brand (so you know the tone rules before you start writing)

You cannot create tasks or use the Allocator — those are lead functions.

---

## Your tasks

Go to **Tasks** in the sidebar. You'll see all tasks assigned to you. Each card shows:
- The deliverable and brand
- Priority (P0 / P1 / P2) and deadline
- The full brief — what the client wants, tone, references, what to avoid
- A **Briefing** badge if the task was generated from a logged client meeting

Read the brief before starting. It has everything Trupti got from the client call.

### Marking status
Update your task as you work:
- **In Progress** — when you start
- **Ready for Review** — when your first draft is done

After "Ready for Review," it goes to Pierre or Pratik for sign-off.

## Brand voice quick ref

Before writing anything, check the brand's tone page in **Brands** → [Brand] → Brand Brain. The rules are tagged by type — tone, visual, hard_no.

| Brand | Copy notes |
|---|---|
| IndiaGate | Premium, warm, heritage. No exclamation marks. No discount language. Short sentences. |
| Vadilal | Fun, accessible, joyful. Family audience. Ice cream: indulgent. Dairy: functional. |
| TrueSilver | Aspirational, human. Not stiff. Speaks to the modern Indian woman. |
| AlphaKid | Two voices: exciting for kids, reassuring for parents. Never corporate-feeling. |
