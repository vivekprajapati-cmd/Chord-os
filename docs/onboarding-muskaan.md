## What you'll use ChordOS for

### Checking what's in production
Go to **Tasks** and filter by brand. You'll see every active deliverable for that brand, its status, priority, and who's working on it. This replaces the "hey what's the status on this?" WhatsApp message.

### Logging client meetings (once POC tier activates)
Go to **Brands** → [Brand] → **+ Log meeting**. Paste your raw call notes — no formatting needed. Claude extracts:
- Summary of what was decided
- Tasks to create (with full briefs)
- New brand rules learned (tone, visual preferences, hard nos)

The extracted rules accumulate in the brand brain and feed into every future task brief automatically.

### Brand Brain
**Brands** → [Brand] → Brand Brain shows everything the system knows about that client — their tone preferences, visual dos and don'ts, and anything they've explicitly rejected. Read this before any client call to refresh your memory on what's already been established.
