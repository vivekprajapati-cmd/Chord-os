## What ChordOS is

ChordOS is the internal ops workspace for 1702 + Chord. It's where all work gets assigned, briefed, and tracked across every brand. For SEO work, this means your deliverables come with a full brief — what the client wants, target keywords, tone, and constraints — without back-and-forth.

## Day-to-day usage

### Allocating SEO work
Go to **Chat** in the sidebar:

> "Block [name] 5 hours this week for IndiaGate SEO blog draft — target keyword 'best basmati rice brands India', P1. Ref: [brief link]"

The system checks availability, applies any relevant brand rules, and notifies the assignee on Slack. Reference link is required.

### Your tasks
**Tasks** page shows all active deliverables. Filter to SEO department or by your name. Each task card shows the full brief including keyword targets, search intent, tone guidance from the brand brain, and deadline.

### Calendar
**Calendar** shows your weekly blocks. Day view with context visible inline.
