## What ChordOS is

ChordOS is the internal ops workspace for 1702 + Chord. It's where all creative work for your brand accounts is tracked — what's in production, what was decided in the last client call, and what the brief says. As Brand Solutions, your input shapes the briefs — this is where that input lives.

## What you'll use ChordOS for

### Checking production status
**Tasks** → filter by brand. See every active deliverable, status, priority, owner. Real-time picture of what's in production without asking anyone.

### Logging client meetings (once POC tier activates)
**Brands** → [Brand] → **+ Log meeting**. Dump raw call notes — any format. Claude extracts:
- What was decided
- Tasks with full creative briefs
- New brand rules (tone, visual, hard nos)

These rules accumulate in the brand brain and auto-feed into every future brief.

### Brand Brain
**Brands** → [Brand] → Brand Brain. Everything the system knows about the client's preferences. Check this before a client call to remember what's already been established and what to avoid repeating.
