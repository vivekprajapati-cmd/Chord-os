## Your tasks

Go to **Tasks** in the sidebar. Each card shows:
- Deliverable and brand
- Priority and deadline
- Full brief
- A **Briefing** badge if the task was generated from a client meeting

### Marking status
- **In Progress** — when you start
- **Ready for Review** — when the first draft or output is done

After "Ready for Review," it goes to Rajat.
