## Logging a client meeting

This is the most important thing you do in ChordOS.

1. Go to **Brands** → [Brand] → **+ Log meeting**
2. Paste your raw notes — no structure needed. Bullet points, fragments, stream of consciousness is fine.
3. Click **Extract** — Claude reads the notes and returns:
   - 2–3 sentence summary of what was discussed
   - Key decisions (flagged by impact level)
   - Suggested tasks with full briefs
   - New brand rules learned
   - Contacts mentioned
4. Review each task. Edit the brief if needed. Set deadlines.
5. Click **Confirm** — meeting is saved, tasks are created, brand knowledge is updated.

Every subsequent meeting only adds *new* rules — it won't duplicate what's already in the brain.
