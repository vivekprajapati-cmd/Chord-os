## Day-to-day usage

### Your tasks
Go to **Tasks** in the sidebar. Filter by your name or by brand. Each task card shows:
- Deliverable name and brand
- Priority (P0 / P1 / P2)
- Your deadline
- The full brief — what the client wants, tone, references, what to avoid
- A **Briefing** badge if the task came from a logged client meeting

### Marking status
Update task status as you move through the work: Scheduled → In Progress → Ready for Review.
When you move a task to "Ready for Review," it goes to Pierre for sign-off.

### Allocating work
Go to **Chat** in the sidebar:
> "Block Yashika 4 hours tomorrow for TrueSilver product post, P2. Ref: [reference link]"

Reference link is required before any task is created.

### Calendar
**Calendar** page shows your blocks for the week. Full day view with context — brand colors, priority, brief visible inline.
