## What ChordOS is

ChordOS is the internal ops workspace for 1702 + Chord. It's where every creative deliverable gets assigned, briefed, tracked, and approved — from the client meeting to the final output. The core problem it solves: knowledge from client calls was dying in WhatsApp. Now it accumulates in a brand brain and flows directly into every brief.

## What you'll use it for day-to-day

### Allocator
Go to **Chat** in the sidebar. Assign work by typing naturally:

> "Block Pratik 6 hours Friday for IndiaGate Diwali concept, P1. Ref: [moodboard link]"

The system checks Pratik's calendar, warns if his load is over 20h active, applies IndiaGate brand rules from the brain, and sends him a Slack notification.

References (mood board, storyboard, Figma link) are required before any task is created.

### Brand Brain
Every brand page has accumulated knowledge from every client meeting ever logged. Rules are tagged: **tone**, **visual**, **process**, **hard_no**.

The Allocator reads this brain automatically. If IndiaGate has a hard_no on discount messaging, it surfaces that rule whenever you assign copy work for that brand.

### Briefings
Chronological feed of all logged meetings across brands. Shows: what was decided, what tasks were generated, what new rules were extracted. Filterable by brand. This is the source of truth for "what did the client say last time?"
