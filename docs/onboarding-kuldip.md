## What ChordOS is

ChordOS is the internal ops workspace for 1702 + Chord. It's where your tasks live — what's assigned to you, the deadline, and the full brief. When you open a task, the creative direction is already there: what the client approved, what the visual mood is, references to draw from.

## Your access level

You are a **staff** member. You can:
- See and update your own tasks
- Mark task status as you progress
- View your calendar blocks
- Read the brand visual language for any brand in the Brand Brain

You cannot create tasks or use the Allocator — those are lead functions.

---

## Your tasks

Go to **Tasks** in the sidebar. Each card shows:
- The deliverable name and brand
- Priority and deadline
- Full brief — motion style, references, brand color palette, what to avoid
- A **Briefing** badge if the task came from a logged client meeting

### Marking status
- **In Progress** — when you start
- **Ready for Review** — when your first output is ready

After "Ready for Review," it goes to Pierre or Pratik.

---

## Calendar

**Calendar** shows your weekly blocks. Check at the start of each day. Full brief visible inline — no need to open the task card separately.
