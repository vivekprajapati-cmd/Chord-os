## What ChordOS is

ChordOS is the internal ops workspace for 1702 + Chord. It tracks every creative deliverable from client meeting to final output. As Brand Solutions, you're the link between what the client says and what gets made — ChordOS is where that link is preserved.

## What you'll use ChordOS for

### Checking production status
**Tasks** → filter by brand. Every active deliverable with status, priority, owner. No need to chase the creative team.

### Logging client meetings (once POC tier activates)
**Brands** → [Brand] → **+ Log meeting**. Raw call notes — any format. Claude extracts decisions, tasks with full briefs, and new brand rules. These accumulate in the brand brain and feed into every future brief automatically.

### Brand Brain
**Brands** → [Brand] → Brand Brain. The full history of the client's preferences, visual rules, and hard nos. Check this before a call. Add to it after.
