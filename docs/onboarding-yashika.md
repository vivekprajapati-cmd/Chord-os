## What ChordOS is

ChordOS is the internal ops workspace for 1702 + Chord. It's where your design tasks live — what's assigned, when it's due, and what the client actually wants. No more waiting to find out what was decided in the meeting. The brief is already in the task.

Login: `yashika.mistry@1702digital.com` — Google SSO.

---

## Your access level

You are a **staff** member. You can:
- See and update your own tasks
- Mark task status as you move through work
- View your calendar blocks
- Read the brand brain for color, typography, and visual rules per brand

---

## Your tasks

Go to **Tasks** in the sidebar. Each card shows:
- Deliverable and brand
- Priority (P0 / P1 / P2) and deadline
- Full brief — format, dimensions, tone, references, what to avoid
- A **Briefing** badge if the task came from a client meeting

Read the brief fully before opening Figma. The visual rules from the brand brain are your constraints.

### Marking status
- **In Progress** — when you start
- **Ready for Review** — first pass done, ready for Pratik or Pierre to look
