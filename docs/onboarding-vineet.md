## What ChordOS is

ChordOS is the internal ops workspace for 1702 + Chord. It's where your video tasks are assigned, briefed, and tracked. When you open a task, the brief is already there — storyboard link, tone direction, what the client approved, what to avoid. You're not waiting on WhatsApp to find out what's needed.

Login: `vineet@1702digital.com` — Google SSO.

---

## Your access level

You are a **staff** member. You can:
- See and update your own tasks
- Mark task status as you progress
- View your calendar blocks
- Read the brand brain for tone and visual rules per brand

---

## Your tasks

Go to **Tasks** in the sidebar. Each card shows:
- The deliverable and brand
- Priority and deadline
- Full brief — cut style, mood, references (storyboard, mood board, drive link), what to avoid
- A **Briefing** badge if the task was created from a client meeting

Before starting any edit, check the brief for the reference links. Every task assigned to you should have at least one — if it's missing, flag Nimesh before starting.

### Marking status
- **In Progress** — when you begin the cut
- **Ready for Review** — when the first cut is done

After "Ready for Review," it goes to Nimesh.

## Calendar

**Calendar** shows your weekly blocks. Nimesh allocates your time here — you'll see tasks appear on your calendar as they're assigned.
