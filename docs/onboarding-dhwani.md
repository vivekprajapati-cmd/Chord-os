## Your tasks

Go to **Tasks** in the sidebar. Each card shows:
- Deliverable name and brand
- Priority and deadline
- Full brief — content angle, tone guidance, references, what to avoid
- A **Briefing** badge if the task was generated from a client meeting

The brief is the single source of truth for what the client wants. Read it before starting.

### Marking status
- **In Progress** — when you start
- **Ready for Review** — when the first draft is ready

After "Ready for Review," it goes to your lead for sign-off.
